Adventures in Fire Island

My trip to Fire Island was amazing and was filled with relaxing beaches, beautiful nature trails, and stunning ocean views. I enjoyed exploring the boardwalks, visiting the historic lighthouse, and watching the sunset over the shoreline. Fire Island is a great destination for anyone looking to enjoy nature and escape the busy city atmosphere.
